Adele Walsh
CERAMIC ARTIST FROM
DUBLIN IRELAND

“I’m Adele – a ceramic designer-maker and a graduate of BA(Hons) in Fine Art; Ceramics and Glass at the National College of Art and Design. My love of nature and found objects are the primary inspiration for my work. Each work emerges through a process of thinking through material responding intuitively to the tactile nature of the materials engaged.”
